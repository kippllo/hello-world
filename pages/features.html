Features

Sentry, designed specifically for Community Corrections, is a multi-user system that enhances staff coordination while maintaining the highest degree of security. The software gives a Residential Center fast and accurate information on its Residents, handles the center's residential Accounting, automating Resident Monitor's duties, and tracks Employment, Education, Medication and Offense information.
